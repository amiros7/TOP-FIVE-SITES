Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy and one of Europe's great cultural cities, is situated in the heart of the Lazio region on the eastern side of the Tiber River. Rome is nothing short of a historical and cultural epicenter, gathering within it the most impressive archaeological monuments from the Roman Empire to the modern day.

Rome is a city of encyclopedic splendor, hosting fine dining restaurants, traditional trattorias and osterias, ancient gateways, as well as beautiful squares like the famous Piazza Navona. Rome also serves as a hub for culture and art, boasting impressive museums such as the Vatican and the National Art Gallery. With decades of urban renewal and revitalization, Rome maintains its reverence for its classical heritage while evolving as a vibrant and dynamic city.
